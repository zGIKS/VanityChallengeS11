Frequently Asked Questions

  • Which Vanity product is for me?
    Vanity has three different products for three different use cases. For a complete comparison and feature list, as well as each supported version of your operating system, check out our product page.
  • What operating system does Vanity support?
    Vanity products are primarily developed for Windows 11 64-bit build 21H2. Compatibility with other Windows versions (e.g., Windows 10) is likely, but Unix-based and 32-bit systems like macOS are not currently supported.
  • Can I get access to beta versions of Vanity Products?
    Access to our beta products is strictly private. Only trusted and loyal users will receive access to beta products. Currently, you cannot receive beta by asking, you must be chosen by our staff.
  • So I've purchased Vanity, now what?
    Usually, our system processes payments instantly, and you'll be redirected to the panel upon completion. However, in some cases, it can take up to 24 hours. If your payment hasn't processed after 48 hours, please submit a support ticket with your transaction ID.
  • Can I share my account with my friends?
    No. Sharing or selling your account will result in it being suspended. If you wish to purchase a subscription for your friend, you can do so by entering their username in the 'Gift' field when purchasing.
  • My Vanity isn't working, what do I do?
    If the client is not opening, or you are getting an error, please try the following:
    - Make sure you are using the latest version of the client.
    - Make sure you are using a supported version for the product.
    - Disable any VPN software you are using temporarily to see if that resolves the issue.
    - If none of the above has resolved your issue, please create a ticket.
